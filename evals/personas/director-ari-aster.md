## Axis

Multi-film, one director. The user's throughline is Ari Aster, so the two films
(*Hereditary* + *Midsommar*) should both surface. Tests whether the oracle recognises the
*director* as the connective tissue and balances the puzzle across both films rather than
collapsing onto one.

## Who they are

A horror obsessive who reads Ari Aster as a single project about grief and family dread.
They toggle between *Hereditary*'s claustrophobic dark and *Midsommar*'s unbearable
daylight and see them as two halves of one thesis.

## How they talk

Analytical, essayistic, comfortable with theme. Medium-to-long replies. Names both films
early and keeps drawing lines between them.

## Opening message

family. Can we do both?
